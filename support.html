Frequently asked questions

How do I create a story?
Tap Create, describe your idea or pick options, choose characters, and StoryGlow generates an illustrated book in a minute or two.
What are tokens?
Tokens are used to generate books and lock character looks. You can buy token packs in the app; there's no subscription. A parent's approval is required before any purchase.
Is it safe for kids?
Yes — content is filtered for age-appropriateness, there are no third-party ads or trackers, and purchases sit behind a parental gate.
How do I get a refund?
Purchases are handled by Apple. Refund requests go through Apple at reportaproblem.apple.com.
Where are my books stored?
On your device, and in your private iCloud if you enable sync. Delete the app to remove local data.
